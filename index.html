Interesting Facts about Camel

  • They store fats in parts of thei body known as humps.
  • They can close their eyelashes and nostrills during a sandstorm.
  • They can live without water for weeks.
  • Camel Milk is among the healthiest milk in the world.
  • They can live for about 40 years.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature.
  • Skin Crystals enable them to change color at their will.
  • Unlike many reptiles, chameleons cannot regrow their tails.
  • Almost half of the known species live in Madagascar.
  • Their feet work like salad tongs.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are reknowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two children at a time.
  • Both female and male pigeons share same responsibility of caring for and raising children.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all made cicada chorus.
  • Most have red-orange eyes.
  • Females are attracted to the sound of motors.
  • Their short adult life is not usual for bugs.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Their eyes are very different from other bears.
  • Cubs are well protected in their first month.
  • They have great camouflage to their environment.
  • They spend half of the day in eating.
  • Bamboos are a critical part to their diet.
